Overview of Plymouth Creek Elementary

Plymouth Creek Elementary is a public school located in Plymouth, MN, which is in a small city setting. The student population of Plymouth Creek Elementary is 671 and the school serves K-5. At Plymouth Creek Elementary, 89% of students scored at or above the proficient level for math, and 86% scored at or above that level for reading. The school's minority student enrollment is 40%. The student-teacher ratio is 15:1, which is better than that of the district. The student population is made up of 53% female students and 47% male students. The school enrolls 5% economically disadvantaged students. There are 44 equivalent full-time teachers.

At Plymouth creek we practice Panther Pride. Students learn what it means to be respectful, responsible, and ready to learn. Staff and students work together to practice empathy, kindness, and our commitment to academic excellence permeates within the walls of each classroom. We know that when students feel a sense of belonging, engaged in solid peer and adult relationships and they receive appropriate academic challenges, they experience success in its fullest capacity. We value the partnership between schools, families, and strive to build those connections everyday to maximize learning for each and every student.
